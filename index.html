Hello and welcome to my page!

My name is Pamela Azzi, but you can call me Pam. I’m a twenty two year old Graphic Designer currently studying my third year at Algonquin College. I speciliaze in Branding, Logo Design, Typography, and Print Media. Although these are my specialties, i’ve worked on everything from Motion Graphics and Animation to HTML/CSS and Web Design.

I love what I do and enjoy working with clients and making their visions come to life. Feel free to check out my portfolio to get a feel of what I do!
